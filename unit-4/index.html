Unit 4: Complete Digital Solutions

Design complete digital solutions and justify decisions through iterative refinement

Unit Overview

In Unit 4, students will design and justify complete digital solutions, synthesizing knowledge from Units 1-3. This capstone unit focuses on end-to-end solution development with iterative refinement.

Key Topics:

  • Complete solution architecture
  • Project management and documentation
  • Stakeholder communication
  • Ethical considerations in digital solutions
  • Deployment and maintenance strategies

Learning Outcomes:

By the end of this unit, you will design, develop, and present a complete digital solution, justifying all design decisions with evidence and user feedback.
